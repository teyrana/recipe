# Herbed Eggplant Slices

- Source: Rosso, Julee. Great Good Food. Crown Publishers, Inc, 1993. p392
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 1 cl garlic, minced
- 1 T fresh minced oregano
- 1/4 C fresh basil leaves
- 1/2 C fresh minced italian parsley
- 1x (1.5lbs / 0.75kg) eggplant
- salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 400F.
2. Lightly spray or wipe a baking sheet with vegetable oil. Set aside.
3. In a small bownl, combine the garlic, oregano basil leaves, and parsely. Mix Well. 
    Set aside.
4. Slice the eggplant into 1/4 rounds.  Generously season each side with salt and pepper. 
    Place on the prepared baking sheet and set on the top rack in the oven. 
5. Bake each side until tender and lightly browned, about 5-7 minutes per side.  Brush each
    eggplant slice with the herbs and place under the broiler 30 seconds.
6. Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.
