# Chili
(Classic Beef Chili)

Yield 10 servings

## Ingredients:
- 2 lbs lean ground beef
- 1(46 fluid ounce) can tomato juice
- 1(29 ounce) can tomato sauce
- 1.5 C chopped onion
- 12 C chopped celery
- 14 C chopped green bell pepper
- 14 C chili powder
- 2C kidney/red beans, drained and rinsed
- 1t salt
- 3cl garlic
- 18 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper


## Directions:

### Beans (if dried):
1. Place beans in a large, heavy pot. 
2. Cover with water about 2” above beans. 
3. Cover pot, bring to a boil, then remove from heat. 
4. Let rest 1 hour. Stir in 1 t kosher salt and bring to a boil over medium heat. 
5. Uncover, reduce heat, and simmer until beans are tender and creamy, checking after 1 hour and adding more water as necessary to keep beans submerged, ( 1 – 1.5 hours total.)

### Stew: 
1. Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet.
Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
2. Add all ingredients to a large kettle. Bring to boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
