# Shrimp Scampi

## Ingredients
- 3T (40g) Butter
- 1/4 C (60g) lemon juice (~1 Lemon)
- 2lb (900g) large shrimp, peeled, deveined, (dried)
- 4 cloves (12g) garlic, minced
- 1/2 C (125g) dry white wine
- 1/4 C (15g) minced fresh parsley
- Salt, pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Saute the shrimp in batches, with oil, (about 2m until opaque).  

2. Saute garlic in melted butter, once fragrant, add wine, cayenne. Stir to blend.

3. Return the shrimp + juices to pan, season with parsley, & salt, and stir to combine.
