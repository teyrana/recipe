# Mexican Vegetarian Tacos

Mexican Vegetarian Tacos. Incredible taste with loads of veggie fiber and protein means this recipe makes you wonder why you ever wanted meat in the first place.


- Prep Time: 15 minutes
- Cook Time: 15 minutes
- Total Time: 30 minutes
- Servings: 6

## Ingredients

- 6 tortillas (ideally corn tortillas)
- 2 T olive oil
- 2 onions
- 1 red bell pepper
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed (1 can = 15.5oz)
- 1 tsp cumin
- salt and pepper to taste
- 8 T cream cheese / sour cream (8 tbsp = 200g)
- 1 lime (for garnish)

### For the Mango Pico de Gallo:
- 1 mid sized avocado
- 0.5 mango
- 1 small onion
- 3 tomatoes, mid-sized
- 0.5 lime, juiced
- 5 sprigs cilantro
- salt to taste


## Instructions
1. Halve and slice the onions into thin half-ring”. Slice the bell pepper into slivers. Saute with olive oil until tender.
2. Now onto the Pico de Gallo: Peel the avocado then mash the flesh in a bowl. Finely dice the onion and cut the tomatoes and mango into small cubes. Combine with lime, salt and chopped cilantro.
3. Add the black beans to the bell pepper and onion, stir and season with cumin, salt, pepper, and garlic. Cook for another 5 minutes, then remove to cool.
4. Heat up the tortillas according to package instructions. To make tacos from tortillas, simply hang them over the wires in your oven for 4-5 mins on 180°C/360°F, then remove.
5. Serve and assemble!
