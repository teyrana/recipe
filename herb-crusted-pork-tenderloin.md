# Herb-Crusted Pork Tenderloin Roast




No store-bought spice rub here. An easy-to-make, homemade rub adds great flavor to the pork!
Submitted by VMB
Prep Time:
10 mins
Cook Time:
45 mins
Additional Time:
10 mins
Total Time:
1 hr 5 mins
Servings:
8
Yield:
8 servings
Ingredients

## Ingredients:
- 1kg / 2.5 lb pork tenderloin roast

### Dry Rub A:

- 4 teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 T rubbed sage
- 2 t dried marjoram
- 1 t salt

### Dry Rub B:

- 3 T olive oil
- 4 teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 t finely grated lemon zest
- 2 t chopped fresh thyme
- 2 t chopped fresh sage
- 2 t chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 t salt
- 1 t coarsely ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Bring roast to room temperature by removing from the refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.

2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).  Cook for 30min / lb

3. Combine olive oil, garlic, thyme, sage, lemon zest, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Spread rub over roast until completely coated. Place on a rack in a roasting pan.

4. Roast in the preheated oven until slightly pink in the center, 45 to 60 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 150 degrees F (66 degrees C).

5. Remove from the oven and tent loosely with foil. Allow to rest for 10 to 15 minutes before carving.
