# Irish Shepherd's Pie

a/o 2026-Apr-13

Prep           25m
Cook        1h 10m
Ready In    1h 35m

## Ingredients

### Mashed Potato Ingredients
- 4 T butter
- 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, chopped 6-8 medium potatoes

### [Primary] Beef filling
- 1 lb lean ground beef | lamb
- 500L Beef Stock
- [1x / 150-200g _fresh_ tomato. (I tried tomato paste, and that was districting.  Maybe try less/or fresh tomato instead?)
- 4 T worcestershire sauce
- 1t - 1T salt / garlic salt
- 1 t ground black pepper (to taste)
- 2 T All purpose Flour 
- 3 onion, diced
- 6 cl garlic, minced
- 2 t minced fresh rosemary
- 12 oz / 1C peas and carrots, diced
- 1 T butter

### Topping
- 1/4 lb Irish cheese (such as Dubliner), shredded

## Directions:
   
1. Preheat the oven to 180C/400F.
2. Cut potatoes into 2" chunks.  Boil for 10-15 mins until tender. Drain, then mash with 85g butter and 3 tbsp milk.
3. Heat 1 T butter or oil in a medium saucepan, then soften chopped onion and chopped carrots for a few mins.
4. Once soft, turn up the heat, add ground lamb/beef, Rosemary, [paprika, if used], thyme, salt, pepper. Brown, tipping off any excess fat.
5. Add Worcestershire sauce and garlic, then fry for a few mins.
6. Pour over beef stock, sprinkle in flour, bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 40 mins, uncovering halfway.
7. Put the filling into an ovenproof dish, top with the mash and ruffle with a fork.
8. (The pie can now be chilled and frozen for up to a month.)
9. Bake for 20-25 mins until the top is starting to colour and the mince is bubbling through at the edges. (To bake from frozen, 150C(?) / 350F (?) 1h 30m or until piping hot in the centre. Flash under the grill to brown, if you like.
