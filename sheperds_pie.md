# Irish Shepherd's Pie

a/o 2026-Apr-13

Prep           25m
Cook        1h 10m
Ready In    1h 35m

## Ingredients

### Mashed Potato Ingredients
- 4 T butter
- 1 C Milk
- 2 lbs Yukon Gold potatoes, chopped 6-8 medium potatoes

### [Primary] Beef filling
- 1 lb lean ground beef | lamb
- 500L Beef Stock
- [1x / 150-200g _fresh_ tomato. (I tried tomato paste, and that was districting.  Maybe try less/or fresh tomato instead?)
- 4 T worcestershire sauce
- 2 t salt / garlic salt
- 1 t ground black pepper
- 2 T All purpose Flour 
- 3 onion, diced (200g?)
- 6 cl garlic, minced
- 2 t minced fresh rosemary
- 2 t Paprika (?)
- 100g Celery, diced
- 100g peas
- 100g carrots, diced
- 1 T butter

### Topping
- 1/4 lb Irish cheese (such as Dubliner), shredded

## Directions:
   
1. Preheat the oven to 180C/400F.
2. Cut potatoes into 2" chunks.  Boil for 10-15 mins until tender. Drain, then mash with 4T (?) butter and 1 C milk.
3. Heat 1 T butter or oil in a medium saucepan, then soften chopped onion, celery, and chopped carrots for 5-8 mins.
4. Once soft, turn up the heat, add ground lamb/beef, Rosemary, [paprika, if used], thyme, salt, pepper. Brown, tipping off any excess fat.
5. Add Worcestershire sauce and garlic, then fry for a few mins.
6. Pour over beef stock, sprinkle in flour, bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for ??? until fully cooked.
7. Put the filling into an ovenproof dish, top with the mash and ruffle with a fork.
8. (The pie can now be chilled and frozen for up to a month.)
9. Bake:
    - From warm: Bake for 20-25 mins until the top is starting to colour and the mince is bubbling through at the edges.
    - From frozen, 150C(?) / 350F (?) for 1h 30m or until piping hot in the centre.
    - [Optional]: Flash under the grill to brown
10. Serve with Cheese
